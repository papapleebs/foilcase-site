Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account?

No. Foilcase works immediately with no sign-up. Your collection is stored on your device.

Does scanning work offline?

Yes. Card recognition runs entirely on your device, so you can scan without an internet connection. You only need a connection for the periodic card-data and price updates.

How often do prices update?

Prices refresh weekly and are shown as estimated market value with an "as of" date. They are meant for tracking trends and estimating collection value, not as live sale prices.

A card scanned as the wrong printing — what do I do?

Tap the card in your review tray and pick the correct printing from the candidate list. Turning on Set Lock for the set you're sorting greatly improves accuracy for bulk entry.

Can I import my collection from another app?

Yes. Go to More → Import to bring in collections and decklists from Moxfield, MTGGoldfish, Arena, ManaBox, Delver, or plain CSV. You can export the same way at any time.

How do I back up or move my collection?

Enable iCloud sync in Settings to mirror your collection across your own devices, and/or use More → Export to save a CSV backup.

Is this an official Wizards of the Coast app?

No. Foilcase is an unofficial companion app made by fans. Card data is used under the Wizards of the Coast Fan Content Policy.
