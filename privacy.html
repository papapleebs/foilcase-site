Privacy Policy

Last updated: [July 21, 2026]

Foilcase does not collect your data.

The short version

Foilcase is designed to be private by default. The app works without an account, recognizes your cards on your device, and stores your collection on your device and in your own iCloud. We ([Your Name / Company]) do not operate a server that receives your personal data, and we do not sell, rent, or share your information with anyone.

Information we do not collect

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, location, contacts, or any advertising identifier. Foilcase contains no third-party advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not track you across apps or websites, so the app never shows an App Tracking Transparency prompt.

Camera

Foilcase uses your device camera to recognize Magic: The Gathering cards. This processing happens entirely on your device. Camera images are analyzed in memory to read a card's set symbol and collector number, and are then discarded. Photos are never uploaded to us or to any third party, and are not saved to your photo library unless you explicitly choose to save one.

Your collection data

The cards, quantities, conditions, purchase prices, notes, folders, and decks you create are stored locally on your device using an on-device database. If you enable iCloud sync, this data is stored in your private iCloud account (Apple's CloudKit private database) so it can sync across your own devices. We have no access to your private iCloud data. Apple's handling of iCloud data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Card data and prices

Foilcase displays Magic: The Gathering card information, images, and estimated market prices sourced from Scryfall and MTGJSON. To keep card data and prices current, the app periodically downloads anonymous, read-only data files from our content delivery network. These requests do not include any account, personal identifier, or information about your collection — they simply fetch the latest public card and price data. Prices are shown as estimated market value for tracking and reference, not as offers to buy or sell.

Purchases

Foilcase is a one-time paid app. Your purchase is processed by Apple through the App Store; we never see your payment details. We do not collect your purchase history.

Links to other services

The app may show links to card vendors (for example, TCGplayer or Card Kingdom). Selecting a link opens that website in Safari. Those sites have their own privacy policies, which we do not control.

Children's privacy

Foilcase is a general-audience utility rated 4+. It does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Data retention and deletion

Because your data stays on your device and in your own iCloud, you are always in control. To delete your data, delete the app; to remove synced data, delete the app's data from iCloud in your device Settings. We hold no server-side copy of your data to delete on your behalf.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, note the change in the app.
