Scan, price, and build your Magic collection.

Foilcase turns your iPhone into the fastest way to catalog, value, and play with your Magic: The Gathering cards — private by default and fully offline for scanning.

Coming to the App Store

Scan in under a second

Point your camera at any card and Foilcase identifies the exact printing — right set, right art, foil or not. On-device and offline.

Track the market

Weekly price updates with dated history, a live value header, and your biggest movers — your gain or loss at a glance.

Build from what you own

Deck builder with Have / Need badges, legality checks, mana-curve charts, and a gap shopping list for what you're missing.

Your data, your way

Import and export Moxfield, MTGGoldfish, Arena, ManaBox, Delver, or CSV. Optional iCloud sync. No account required.
